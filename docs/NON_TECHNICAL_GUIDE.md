# Elite Mail System - Non-Technical Overview

**A Simple Guide to Understanding the Elite Mail Platform**

---

## What is Elite Mail?

Elite Mail is a **subscription-based email management platform** that helps customers get email inboxes set up for their business needs. Think of it like ordering a service where you tell us how many email addresses you need, and our team creates them for you.

---

## How Does It Work? (Simple 5-Step Process)

### Step 1: Customer Signs Up
- Customer visits the website
- Chooses a subscription plan (like picking a phone plan)
- Pays through our secure payment system (Chargebee)
- Creates an account

### Step 2: Order is Created
- System automatically creates an order
- Order goes into a queue (like a waiting line)
- Admin or Team Leader reviews the order

### Step 3: Work is Assigned
- Admin assigns the order to a Contractor (our team member)
- Contractor receives a notification
- Contractor starts working on the order

### Step 4: Emails are Created
- Contractor generates the email addresses
- Emails are organized in batches (groups of 200)
- Everything is stored securely in our system

### Step 5: Customer Receives Access
- Customer gets notified when order is complete
- Customer can download their email addresses
- Customer can access support if needed

## Important System Concepts (Simplified)

### Order Statuses
Orders move through different stages:

1. **Pending** - Order just created, waiting to be assigned
2. **Draft** - Assigned to contractor but not started
3. **In Progress** - Contractor is working on it
4. **Completed** - All done, customer can access emails
5. **Cancelled** - Order was cancelled
6. **Rejected** - Contractor couldn't complete it (goes back to queue)

### Pool Orders
**What's different:** Instead of regular orders, pool orders work with customer's own domain list
- Customer provides a list of domains
- System allocates domains to "pool panels"
- Emails are created for each domain

**Use case:** For customers with many domains who need emails for all of them.

### Domain Removal
**What it is:** Cleaning up when subscriptions end
- When customer cancels, we schedule domain removal
- Gives customer 30 days to backup their data
- System automatically removes domains after 30 days
